Jared Carthalion

Jared Carthalion is a versatile card in Magic: The Gathering, often used as a planeswalker with abilities centered around creating and enhancing creature tokens while providing board-wide effects. As a commander or part of your main deck, Jared shines in decks focused on multicolor synergies and token strategies. His abilities often reward you for controlling permanents of multiple colors, offering bonuses like distributing +1/+1 counters, creating tokens, or protecting your board. To maximize Jared's potential, build your deck with creatures and spells that utilize a variety of colors. Enchantments like Prismatic Bridge or artifacts like Chromatic Lantern can help stabilize your mana base for activating Jared's abilities effectively. He works well with cards that benefit from or create tokens, such as Doubling Season or Parallel Lives. In gameplay, Jared can create pressure by growing your battlefield while reinforcing your creatures with counters or lifelink effects, making him an excellent tool for midrange strategies. He also pairs well with cards that offer color flexibility, enabling you to meet his multicolor conditions consistently.

As a six-mana, five-color 6/6 legendary dragon spirit with flying and trample, Okagachi is both a strong attacker and a deterrent. Its ability triggers when an opponent deals damage to you, allowing Okagachi to attack that player and exile one of their permanents. Faeburrow Elder

Faeburrow Elder is a highly versatile creature in Magic: The Gathering, ideal for decks that focus on multicolor strategies and mana ramp. As a 0/0 green and white creature with vigilance, Faeburrow Elder enters the battlefield with +1/+1 for each color among permanents you control. Its standout feature is its ability to tap for one mana of each color among permanents you control, making it a potent mana dork in decks with three or more colors. To make the most of Faeburrow Elder, pair it with cards that enhance its mana-producing potential, such as enchantments like Utopia Sprawl or artifacts like Chromatic Lantern. Since it scales with the number of colors in your permanents, include a diverse array of multicolored cards and permanents like Knight of Autumn or General Ferrous Rokiric. Buffing Faeburrow Elder with enchantments like Rancor or Shield of the Oversoul can increase its power and survivability while giving you a sturdy attacker or blocker. In gameplay, use Faeburrow Elder as an early ramp piece to accelerate your game plan, casting high-cost spells or enabling activated abilities of multicolored permanents. Its vigilance also allows it to contribute to both offense and defense, ensuring value every turn.
